💡 What the parser understands
Dates: 5/16, 05/16/2026, 2026-05-16, "May 16 2026"  •  Amounts: $50.53, -50.53, (50.53), 50.53 DR/CR
Auto-typing: negative or "debit" → Spending, positive or "credit/deposit" → Extra Income, "reward/cashback" → Rewards
Investments: recognizes ticker symbols (QQQ, SPY), share counts, buy/sell prices
Timesheets: recognizes HH:MM hours and date ranges
CSV: reads headers (Date, Description, Amount, Type, Category, Hours, Rate, etc.) automatically

About

MyFinances v1.0

All data is stored locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server.

Works on mobile, tablet, and desktop. Install as a PWA from your browser's share/install option.

To host on GitHub Pages: push index.html to a repo and enable Pages in Settings.
